My Diploma in Electrical Engineering result is still not published. Instead of wasting this waiting period, I made a decision: use this time wisely by doing real field practice at my uncle's electrical shop.

## Classroom vs Field

In Bangladesh, polytechnic and training centers often fall short on the practical side. Theory is taught well, but hands-on skills? That's a different story.

> Working directly in the field is a completely different experience. Every connection, every fault, every fix teaches something new.
